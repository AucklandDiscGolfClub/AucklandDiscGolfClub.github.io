Hall of fame

When the club was started the first 100 numbers were earmarked for future use

The Auckland Disc Golf Club has recently established the Hall of Fame to honor outstanding individuals who have made significant contributions to the sport of disc golf in Auckland New Zealand.

Annually the process begins with member nominations, which are then reviewed and evaluated by the club committee.
