Membership Types:

The club has 2 membership tiers, Active and Free,

Membership year runs the calendar year, January -> December.

If an Active member does not renew for the new membership year they revert to the Free membership tier.

Active
$25 / year
  • Lifetime Membership Number
  • Discount to all club events
  • First access to club events
  • Voting Rights
  • Ability to stand for committee positions
  • Membership Year - January 1st to December 31st (new memberships started after October will extend into the next year)
  • Bag Tag
Free
$0 / year
  • Lifetime Membership Number
  • Relevant Club Emails
