Auckland has a range of various courses.

Council park courses.

Henderson

10 Basket course, with short and long tees

In summer during daylight savings usually has a Weekly Wednesday League.

Rosedale

9 hole course

accessible course on the North Shore

Comercial

Woodhill

18 hole with blue and red Teepad locations + additional putting course

Sandy based ground with low grass, providing playability even in wet wintery months.

Formerly had a additional Black 9 course within the pines providing a difficult wooded challenge for advanced players

Regional Park

Pae o te rangi

18 hole course with green and blue tees, and a shorter beginner layout for the campsite.

Waitawa

18 hole and 9 hole basketed courses

18 hole with green and blue tees and an alternate layout for championship. (combines hole 1+2 into a par 5 and a basket over a valley)

Champange 9 is a more approachable course while still providing difficulty, and picturesque sea views.

Other

Hobsonville Point High School

A 6 hole course

Course is on school grounds, restricting access to outside of school hours and when the sports fields aren't in use.

Waiheke

Situated at Onetangi sports park is Aucklands first permanently basketed course, course has ambiguous tees, and potentially missing or damaged baskets in places, several of the holes may be unsafe to play when the sports fields are in use.

Bella Rakha

Bella is a Private course, on the Retreat centre,

Don't play/show up, without prior permission!

Occasionally will hold working bees, or be used for tournaments.
