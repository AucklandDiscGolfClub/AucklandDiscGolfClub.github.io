Bag Tags

Bag tags are competed for between tag owners, the goal being to try claim a lower numbered tag

Typically tags are played player vs player within a card/group of players. with the lowest scored member of the card getting the lowest number in the group, second getting the second lowest ..., in the case of a tie who had the lowest tag previous usually wins, although some players will do playoff holes for the lower tag.

Special rules may occur at events, like "all tags in" where everyone hands in their tag at the beginning of the event, and the tag you get back at the end is assigned by your event position result
