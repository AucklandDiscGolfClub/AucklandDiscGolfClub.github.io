After years of talking about creating a club for the Auckland community, Allen Gorthy volunteered to start the process beginning to take membership, drafting club rules, and organising an election to establish a committee, in December of 2020 a couple of weeks after the NZDG Matchplay nominations were opened for the club committee to formally establish an Auckland Club.

End of January 2021 election and General meeting confirmed a committee and they proceeded to officially form a club

The club was officially Registered as an Incorporated Society on the 1st of March 2021.
